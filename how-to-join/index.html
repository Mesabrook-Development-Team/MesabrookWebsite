General Availability Status:

Mesabrook is currently whitelisted

How to Join

Connect using mesabrook.com

Mesabrook is currently whitelisted, meaning only approved players can join it. When the server is ready to be opened to the public fully, any player running the Mesabrook Modpack will be able to freely join.

Minimum System Requirements

Due to the size and complexity of our Modpack, we recommend players connect only if their computers meet the following requirements:

  • CPU: Quad-Core Intel, AMD, or Apple M1
  • Memory: At least 8 GB.
  • GPU: Must support OpenGL.
  • OS: Windows 7/8.x/10/11, macOS, or Linux.
  • Java: Java 8

While you may be able to play on Mesabrook with a system meeting these requirements, your playing experience may not be the best. We recommend having a somewhat-modern system.
