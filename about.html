What exactly is Mesabrook?

Mesabrook is a modded Minecraft server that focuses heavily on realism and logistics. When we open, Mesabrook will be home to complex networks and supply chains that sustain real world-styled businesses and industry.

There will be lots of things for players to do and the complexity of our modpack will ensure that your time playing on Mesabrook is never boring!

We're not like any other modded survival server. Mesabrook is its own experience!
