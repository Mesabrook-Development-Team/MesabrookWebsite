About Mesabrook

What exactly is Mesabrook?

Mesabrook is a modded Minecraft server that focuses heavily on realism and logistics. When we open, Mesabrook will be home to complex networks and supply chains that sustain real world-styled businesses and industry.

There will be lots of things for players to do and the complexity of our modpack will ensure that your time playing on Mesabrook is never boring!

We're not like any other modded survival server. Mesabrook is its own experience!

About the Founding Council

The Founding Council consists of the Mesabrook server's highest-ranking staff members, including the server's founder.

CSX8600

Adam Clussman (aka CSX8600) is the main founder of Mesabrook. He is the Head Councilor of the Republic of Mesabrook and is the Owner/Operator of

  • Iron River & Western Railroad
  • Iron River Power
  • Iron River Security Solutions

Adam is also involved in the Mesabrook Nuclear power station located in Ravenholm City. He is also the Mayor of Iron River, Mesabrook's capital city.

He is also Mesabrook's Head Developer.

TrainDevil

Levi Carlson (aka TrainDevil) is a member of the Founding Council. He is the Owner/Operator of

  • Leviathan LLC. and all of its subsidiaries

He is also Mesabrook's main artist and has designed over sixty pieces of Mesabrook-themed rolling stock for the server. He also holds the official position of Modifications Secretary with his role being the overall management of the Mesabrook Modpack.

RavenholmZombie
